Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, information and more

Jojo's an anime that consist of 8 parts, each varying on different protagonist. Each protagonist from their respective parts are "Jojo's" from different generations. So a new, unique story can always be told.

 In Jojo's bizarre adventure, the power system is based upon two powers. Initially it was hamon, where the user used a breathing techinque to enhance their physical strength. And now the current power is Stands, a "ghost" that a user can control and each can have different abilities.
