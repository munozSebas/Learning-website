Demon Slayer and its uprise

Following the story of Tanjiro Kamado, whose sister has become a demon. He seeks to find a cure to convert her back into a human But along the way he has to become a demon slayer to come a litte closer in accomplishing his goal.

With an amazing group of side characters such as Inosuke and the Hashiras, Demon slayer to this day was the anime that slowly brought me back into anime and reading manga. It always keeps me on the edge of my seat.

After reading the mangas my favorite character of the series is Obanai Iguro, his sword, style, and everything about him is what makes him my favorite character.
