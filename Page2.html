Dorohedoro and its amazing art

I recently started Dorohedoro, the story as of now, is about two worlds. The HOLE and the world where sorcerers live . The sorcerers test on the people who live in the HOLE. The protagonist is a half caiman and half man, who seeking who the sorcerer who transformed him

 The mangaka of Dorohedoro is a woman, her art style and griddiness is what caught my attention. And lead me to read her story in the first place.

The main character of the story Caiman, is resistent to magic, which makes him harder to defeat. And as a Caiman, he is able to regenerate his body parts as well. His favorite food is Gyozas.
