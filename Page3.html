One Piece and its greatness

The highest selling manga of all time, One Piece exceeded my expectations, a simple story about a young pirate Monkey D Luffy striving to become the Pirate King and to find the one piece.

 Although the mangas has over 100 volumes and the still running anime has over 1000 episodes, many have are up to date to it. Myself included the idea of One Piece being pirates and going on constant advetures is what always keeps it fresh.

With the constant adventures, we are introduced to amazing side characted. My favorite at the moment being Sanji. And the people I look forward to seeing is DonFlamingo and Law.
